# Petition Flow

1. Contributor signs the civic petition.  
   [Sign the Petition](https://www.change.org/p/join-the-movement-sign-the-petition-to-regulate-digital-currencies-in-egypt)

2. Petition entry is added to `registry/petitions.json`.  
3. Badge assignment begins at "Citizen" tier.  
4. Validator reviews and logs in `chapter_resurrection.md`.  
5. Contributor gains access to onboarding scripts and vault view.
