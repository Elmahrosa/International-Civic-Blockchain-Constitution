## Purpose

The **Preliminary Access Inquiry (PAI) Receipt** is a formal cryptographic
acknowledgment issued **prior to** any technical access, data sharing,
or execution capability.

It confirms that a ministry or sovereign authority has formally reviewed
and acknowledged:

- The **International Civic Blockchain Constitution (ICBC)**
- The **TEOS Sovereign License (TESL)**

This acknowledgment is mandatory before any further engagement.

---

## What the PAI Receipt Does

✔ Confirms official institutional acknowledgment  
✔ Creates an immutable, timestamped audit record  
✔ Prevents informal or undocumented onboarding  
✔ Protects national sovereignty and jurisdictional authority  
✔ Enables transparent oversight and accountability  

---

## What the PAI Receipt Does *Not* Do

✘ It does **not** authorize execution  
✘ It does **not** move funds or assets  
✘ It does **not** grant automation or AI control  

All execution requires a **separate Authorization Stamp**
issued under constitutional authority.

---

## Legal and Technical Guarantees

- Cryptographically signed by authorized officials  
- Timestamped and immutable once issued  
- Auditable via on-chain anchoring  
- Governed by constitutional law  
- Non-repudiable and legally defensible
