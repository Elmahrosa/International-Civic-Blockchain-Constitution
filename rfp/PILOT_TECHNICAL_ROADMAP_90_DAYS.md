## 0. Purpose of the Pilot

The 90-day pilot validates **governance, compliance, and sovereignty**, not scale.

The objective is to demonstrate that a nation can:
- Operate digital infrastructure under its **own law**
- Maintain **full data sovereignty**
- Deploy DPI without vendor dependency
- Produce **audit-grade evidence** for funding and scale decisions

This pilot is **not** a public launch and **not** a production rollout.
