## 2. What TEOS Is (and Is Not)

### TEOS Is:
- A **sovereign software framework** for national digital infrastructure
- Constitutionally governed and law-aligned
- Deployable as a **single-country instance**
- Fully owned and operated by the adopting state

### TEOS Is Not:
- A vendor-controlled platform
- A centralized global network
- A data-extractive technology
- An equity or token sale mechanism

TEOS is designed to **serve governments**, not to replace them.
